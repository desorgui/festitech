    Main Accessibility

    • Parking Services

      Parking is available at several lots located in downtown. All lots include designated ADA parking spaces. Pre-paid parking can be purchased HERE.

    • Animal Services

      Service animals are the only animals allowed onto the festival grounds. Emotional support animals do not qualify as service animals.

    • Medical Services

      First aid locations are offered throughout the venue. Please refer to your festival map, or contact the nearest staff member.

    • Amenities Services

      All public restroom areas, ATMs and concession stands are accessible to our patrons with disabilities. Please see your festival map for locations.

    • View

      DLVEC provides an accessible, designated viewing area at each stage.
